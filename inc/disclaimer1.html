This website shows the results of an ongoing activity of the EU project RISE (Horizon 2020 n. 821115). The technical background is summarised to link here
Presently, the system replicates only the authoritative ShakeMap configurations for Italy and Switzerland whereas a general configuration for the rest of the Euro-Mediterranean region is used.
The website is experimental and the developers of this system do not have a mandate to backup any other national systems. Yet, additional national / regional authoritative contributions are strongly encouraged.
Since the project is under development and optimisation, the content of the websites and of the maps can change without any notice. That is, this is a prototype stillunder development which may change at any time without notice. In addition, the products are not warranted to be correct, accurate, or even functional.
